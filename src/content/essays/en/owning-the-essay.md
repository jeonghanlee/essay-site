Social platforms are useful for announcing new work, but they are weak as the primary home for long-form essays. The format pulls writing toward the feed, and the feed is not designed for stable revision, citation, or long memory.

This site treats Git as the source of publication. Korean and English essays are managed as independent originals, connected only when they share a durable topic identity. A missing counterpart is not an error.

LinkedIn, Facebook, and X remain useful, but only as announcement channels. Each post should carry a title, a short note, and a link back to the canonical page.

The goal is not to make writing heavier. The goal is to remove repeated posting work and keep the essay itself at the center.
